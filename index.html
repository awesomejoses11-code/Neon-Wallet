Frequently Asked Questions

Is my private key safe?

Yes. Your private keys are generated and stored locally on your device. They never leave your browser and are never transmitted to any server. All transactions are signed client-side.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. Connect your existing TON wallet or create a new one directly in the app. Everything is non-custodial.

What networks are supported?

Currently TON blockchain. Ethereum, Polygon, Solana, and other chains are planned for Phase 3.

Is there a mobile app?

The web app is fully mobile-optimized and installable as a PWA on both iOS and Android. Native apps may come later.

Are there any fees?

The wallet is free to use. Transaction fees are determined by the blockchain network, not by us.
